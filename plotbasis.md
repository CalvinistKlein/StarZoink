The Core gameplay premise: The player is orbiting a remote gas giant in the outer rim. They are the commander of the Star Destroyer, given the rank of Commodore by the Emperor himself. The player acts as a special agent for the Emperor, performing his bidding in the Outer Rim. They report directly to the Emperor, and have special technologies and ship upgrades not found on standard star destroyers. This includes a faster hyperdrive and more powerful shields and weapons. You also have an Inquisitor assigned to your ship to help and keep an eye on the operation. 

The Mission: Investigate an ancient Sith beacon resonating from deep space. This could be an asteroid with a space station built into it, or a derelict ship of some kind. Recover any artifacts and return any information to the Emperor directly. 

Combat is turn-based and tactical. It uses the FFG Star Wars RPG combat system for mechanics, but with simplified rules for the LLM to handle. 

Skills: Athletics, Piloting, Astrogation, Computers, Engineering, Medicine, Perception, Persuasion, Stealth, Thievery, Survival, Tactics, Vehicles, Weapons, Blaster Rifles, Unarmed Combat, Melee Weapons, Heavy Weapons, Ranged Weapons.
